WARRIoRS

The Elementary School Teams compete in the Ice Lines in-house ESL. The players are seprated into two to three teams to compete in divisions based on skill set.  Unlike the MS and HS teams the ES teams practice and games are held on Saturday and Sundays. The league is run by Ice Line and the clubs are assinged players based on the feeder elementary school they attend

Games

The games are held Saturday and Sunday mornings

Practice

The teams practice on Saturday and Sunday mornings

Having Fun

The goal of the ES program is to allow the players to learn, build skill and have fun with the game of ice hockey.
